What is Σtockly?

Σtockly helps you make informative decisions as to what actions to make given a stock's price, Twitter sentiment and careful analysis from our machine learning model.

Features

Analyze Stocks Recommend Portfolios Market Projections Asset Protection
Analyze Stocks

Stock.ly provides a live analysis of every stock across a number of markets to provide you with all of the information you need to make prudent decisions with your investment capital.

Recommend Portfolios

Are you looking at making a new investment, or expanding your portfolio to include different stocks than your current fund? Stock.ly's smart-learning behavior can look at market trends to recommend different options based on things like item growth, risk potential, and other features!

Market Projections

Because Stock.ly is capable of making trajectory projections for stocks, it can also use its learning to help the user better understand the current path of index funds, like the S&P 500 or DOW Jones.

Asset Protections

You can set up your Stock.ly account to include options like our Asset Protection feature, where you will be automatically alerted if your market value drops below a specific threshold for an extended period of time.
